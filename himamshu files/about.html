The Challenge

Understanding the crisis facing Indian Class 11-12 students

Academic Stress Crisis

1 in 5 high-schoolers rarely feels calm, motivated, or excited. Nearly 75% of 12th graders report sleeping less than 6 hours, with 70% experiencing moderate to severe stress levels.

Exam Pressure

High-stakes board exams and entrance tests, combined with parental expectations, create overwhelming pressure. Students internalize the belief that "if I don't top my exam, I've failed in life."

Poor Time Management

Excessive workload, procrastination, and lack of structured planning lead to cramming, sleep loss, and increased anxiety. Many students keep tasks in their heads rather than using planners.

Lack of Guidance

Nearly 40% of students don't know where to get professional help at school. Schools often lack counselors or structured study guidance, leaving students to cope alone.

Our Solution

How BUDDIQ addresses these challenges

Personalized Study Plans

Automatically allocates study hours per subject, balanced by difficulty and upcoming tests. Our adaptive scheduling algorithm adjusts to each student's performance, ensuring no topic is overlooked.

Dynamic Rescheduling

As students complete tasks or struggle with topics, the schedule updates itself. Harder topics get more spaced repetitions over time, while easier ones require less review.

Chunking & Break Reminders

Breaks study time into 45-60 minute blocks with built-in 5-10 minute breaks, exactly as educational experts recommend to reduce fatigue and procrastination.

Habit Creation Tools

Students set small daily goals and track consistency through streaks. Research shows that forming regular habits reduces cognitive load and stress over time.

Progress Analytics

Provides comprehensive stats on study time, completed tasks, and sleep patterns. Visualizing progress helps students feel more in control and less anxious.

AI-Powered Assistance

Our intelligent chatbot provides instant answers, explanations, and study guidance, available 24/7 to support students whenever they need help.
